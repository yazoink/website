Languid black dust in the night... Glimmer of police lights in the bathroom window...


Twinges of metal and vague fireworks... A face comprised of bright two-dimensional shapes -- sacred geometry of fear... Tracing the sinking feeling of the gut with stale yellow fingers...


Imaginary astral projection through the cool shiny window, fly-screen torn. Old glass bottle on the shelf stained with red Castlemaine dirt -- maybe 1856...


Chemical effluvium of bottom-shelf gin and home-brand tonic. Smooth round ideas contort and tremble -- Neck cracks like a glowstick on Christmas Eve...


Nostalgia of twinkling LED lights, all along the boulevard -- grown a dusty brown exoskeleton... The scene stained with bright electrical static and lingering patches of light...


Traces of unknown guilt -- dubious origin... Hands involuntarily surround face -- going in for the catch... Portable fan staring directly at me with its big indifferent grey eye -- sharp bladed iris.


Wincing precarious flesh -- Warm January moonlight gleams in humid malevolence...
